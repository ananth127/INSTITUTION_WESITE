Welcome To
VSB Engineering College!!

V.S.B. Educational Trust was founded in the year 2000 by Mr. V.S. Balsamy, the founder and correspondent of the V.S.B. Engineering College, with an interest in promoting, managing and administrating educational institutions with high academic standards, discipline and to take up and help other allied activities in the field of education. Under the Trust, V.S.B. Engineering College,Karur was established in the year 2002 and V.S.B College of Engineering Technical Campus,Coimbatore in the year 2012. Our College is located on NH-67 about 13 kms from Karur on road and spread across 32.28 acres of lush of green campus with 4,50,000 sq. ft. built-up area.Physically, it is dotted with majestic ,arterial roads, green carpeted lawns and brimming trees.
